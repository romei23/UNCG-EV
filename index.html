The UNCG EV’s website improves the EV charging experience for faculty, students, and staff by addressing inefficiencies in the first-come, first-serve system. It provides real-time charger availability, allows users to book time slots, and sends reservation notifications. By reducing wait times and optimizing charger use, the website enhances convenience and supports UNCG’s sustainability efforts.
